etg.simulation.company module¶

All the classes and methods having to do with the companies in the game.

class etg.simulation.company.Company(simulation, options, name)[source]¶

Bases: etg.simulation.entity.Entity

A class to represent the companies in the simulation. It contains information about the state of the company (funds, suppliers, consumers, etc) and methods for updating the company and validating user input. The companies are created when a player joins, and should not be created manually.

buyers¶

The number of users of this company.

demand¶

The amount of energy that the consumers of this company require

donate(party_name, amount)[source]¶

Donate the amount to the party with party_name. Returns True if succesful, False with an error message otherwise.

income¶

How much money the company earned this tick.

market_share¶

The percentage of the population that buys their energy here.

output¶

The output of this company on the current tick.

product_cost¶

How much the product from this company costs.

product_green¶

How green the product of this company is.

product_safety¶

How safe the product of this company is.

profit¶

How much profit the company makes each tick.

rawcost¶

How much the product of the company costs the company itself.

sell(producer_name)[source]¶

Sell the producer with producer_name for half of the money of the tier that it is at

set_values(config)[source]¶

Set all the values on this company by coping them from the config dict from the simulation.

taxes¶

The amount of taxation that goes over this product, calculated by taking the proportions for all the energy types in the output.

tick()[source]¶

In a tick, the companies do their marketing, by updating the needs of the agents to mimic their product.

total_output¶

The output and market per producer.

upgrade(producer_name)[source]¶

Upgrade the producer with producer_name, if the budget for that is available. Return True on success, False with an error message otherwise.

users¶

All the users of this comapny, as an AgentSet.
